Instructions:
You have just sent 2 opponents to the arena. The arena is proudly sponsored, where the sponsor makes the match possible. No kittens are harmed - but all prey animals stand the chance of being destroyed forever, so be careful on how many prey animals you send against the top feline warriors.

To play, select 2 animals from the front page, the first animal you select is the one you control in the event, the other will defend itself with the skill it has learned in previous bouts. By pressing the control buttons at the top of the arena you encourage and direct your animal, giving it stamina, power and perrrrrrfection for a perfect fight.

At the end of the round, you can restart a battle from the home screen. you may need to select 'more cats' to summon the warrior you are training to continue it's quest. You can abandoned training of a particular warrior at any time and start training another - but a warrior will lose expereince if it is not participating in events.

Notice: Cats are in timeout for bad behavior, and will be available to return to the matches when they have completed their sensitivity training on no eye gouging, claw yanking, or tail biting. check back shortly
