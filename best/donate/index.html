Donate History

Up until around the 18'th century, most charities were managed through the church or religious institution of the state. We now have a charity, charitable organization, non-profit organization known as an NPO. Often called foundations, charitable trust.
From the IRS 501C3 publication, Charitable organizations are identified as follows: * Relief of the poor, the distressed, or the underprivileged,
* Advancement of religion,
* Advancement of education or science,
* Erection or maintenance of public buildings, monuments, or works,
* Lessening the burdens of government,
* Lessening of neighborhood tensions,
* Elimination of prejudice and discrimination,
* Defense of human and civil rights secured by law, and
* Combating community deterioration and juvenile delinquency.
