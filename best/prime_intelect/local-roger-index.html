The "Jacket Copy"

Lawrence had ordained that Prime Intellect could not, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm. But he had not realized how much harm his super-intelligent creation could perceive, or what kind of action might be necessary to prevent it.

Caroline has been pulled from her deathbed into a brave new immortal Paradise where she can have anything she wants, except the sense that her life has meaning.

Now these two souls are headed for a confrontation which will force them to weigh matters of life and death before a machine that can remake -- or destroy -- the entire Universe.

Dead Tree Project
When I first put this novel online I had many requests to have it published on paper, but the Print On Demand industry was still in its infancy demanding stupid fees for poor results. Eventually I had it published by Lulu.com, which was the first POD publisher to not require ridiculous setup fees. Today you can get Prime Intellect and a number of my other writings in both print and eBook format from Amazon; see the main site at localroger.com or the author/fan blog at Passages in the Void or just put one of my titles into the Amazon search bar for more info.
