Utilities History

Historically, infrastructure has been developed around energy and functional requirements to life including Coal, Electricity, natural gas, telephone, sewer, water, transportation and in more modern times broadband internet


Types of utilities

Coal

Historically Coal was the primary source of heat after wood, but in modern times coal is rarely used for home heating and instead is one of the legacy forms of power generation. Companies using this today include:
* Duke energy
* Reliant Energy
* Georgia Power


Electricity

Nearly every populated region in the Uniited States has at least one utility that serves electricty to habitable areas. The Top 10 utiliites by volume in the U.S. are:
* Pacific Gas & Electric
* Southern California Edison
* Florida Power & Light
* Consolidated Edison
* Georgia Power
* Dominion Energy
* DTE Energy
* Public Service Elec & Gas
